The looks have changed, functionality is the same. The design is better accommodated to various display devices. More at Central Library news.
What can you find in Primo?

Primo is a discovery service that allows you to find information and resources for your study or scientific work. You can find here books, journals and other resources from all the libraries of Brno University of Technology. Significant part of the portfolio consists of electronic journals and books as well as electronic versions of technical reports, theses and many others.

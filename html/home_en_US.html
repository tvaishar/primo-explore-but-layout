Effects of anti-virus measures on the library system

Because of the closure of all BUT libraries, we have undertaken following measures to mitigate negative effects on our users:

  • Loans which were due from 11. 3. to 31. 3. were prolonged until 1.4.
  • All the libraries were closed also in the library system, which means that all running fines should not be increasing during the aforementioned period.

We will react to the situation and eventually extend the measures further.
What can you find in Primo?

Primo is a discovery service that allows you to find information and resources for your study or scientific work. You can find here books, journals and other resources from all the libraries of Brno University of Technology. Significant part of the portfolio consists of electronic journals and books as well as electronic versions of technical reports, theses and many others.
